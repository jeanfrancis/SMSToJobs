Thanks for visiting!

txt2wrk was founded in Oakland, CA to improve access to employment opportunities for people with low literacy and limited access to broadband.

txt2wrk was awarded the Ramsell Prize for the best app at the Code for Oakland one-day hackathon on June 4, 2011.
